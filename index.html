Biography

  • 2020-Current Assistant Professor in College of Electronic and Information Engineering, Shenzhen University, Shenzhen
  • 2018-2020 Senior Researcher in Financial Technology Group, Tencent, Shenzhen
  • 2018-2018 Postdoc Research Fellow in Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), Hong Kong
  • 2013-2018 Ph. D. in Information Engineering, CUHK, Hong Kong (Thesis: Demand Response Strategy in Electricity Market: an Energy Storage System Perspective)
  • 2009-2013 Bachelor of Enigneering in Information Enigneering, CUHK, Hong Kong (Thesis: Automatic trading systems: a pattern recognition approach)

Shuoyao Wang received the B.Eng. degree (with first class Hons.) and the Ph.D degree in information engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, in 2013 and 2018, respectively. From 2018 to 2020, he was an senior researcher with Financial Technology Group, Tencent, Shenzhen, China. Since 2020, he has been with the College of Electronic and Information Engineering, Shenzhen University, Shenzhen, China, where he is currently an Assistant Professor. His research interests include optimization theory, operational research, and machine learning in Multimedia Processing, Smart Grid, and Communications.
